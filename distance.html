  • Non-Virus Deaths in India

People walked long distances in the hope to reach their homes. In several cases, over many days. We look at 27 exhaustion related instances where a person (or a group) traveled to their home cities/towns/villages.

About Paths

  • Each path represents the total distance a person (or a group of people) attempted to cover by a combination of transportation means.
  • represents distance covered before they passed away. represents total distance.
  • Distances are approximated to nearest number.
