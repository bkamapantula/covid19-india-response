Who They Are(current) Where They Died
  • Non-Virus Deaths in India

During the Covid-19 driven lockdowns (different phases) there have been hundreds of reports of deaths related to starvation, exhaustion, police harassment, suicide from financial distress among many other reasons.

People died all over the country.

About Map

  • Each dot represents an incident. Each incident can involve one or more deaths.
  • Dot size represents number of deaths in one incident.
  • Hover on dot or Click it to know more.
